About the Game

Brother To Me is a two-player cooperative puzzle-adventure inspired by classic co-op titles. Play as two siblings who must work together to solve inventive puzzles, traverse whimsical levels, and rebuild their bond. Created and showcased by the Tamil Gaming YouTube channel.

About Tamil Gaming

The Tamil Gaming தமிழ் கேமிங் YouTube channel is recognized as India’s first and one of the pioneering Tamil gaming channels. It is run by two brothers, Vivek and Prem, who have been creating gameplay commentary in Tamil for over 15 years. Their channel features a variety of popular games with entertaining and engaging story-driven content in the Tamil language, which has helped them build a large, dedicated community. They initially started with a previous channel called Vivek & Prem Gamers before launching Tamil Gaming. The channel has achieved significant milestones, including millions of subscribers and extensive viewership, making it an influential presence in the Tamil gaming community in India.
