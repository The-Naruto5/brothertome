About the Game

Brother To Me is a two-player cooperative puzzle-adventure inspired by classic co-op titles. Play as two siblings who must work together to solve inventive puzzles, traverse whimsical levels, and rebuild their bond. Created and showcased by the Tamil Gaming YouTube channel.

About Tamil Gaming

Tamil Gaming (தமிழ் கேமிங்) is one of India’s first Tamil gaming channels, created by brothers Vivek and Prem. With 15+ years of gameplay commentary, story-driven content, and millions of viewers, it has become a major part of the Tamil gaming community.
